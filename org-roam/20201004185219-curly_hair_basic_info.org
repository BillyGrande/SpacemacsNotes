#+title: Curly Hair Basic Info

#+tags: No_sulfates, No_silicones

Curly hair are dry by default. Modern shampoos use sulfates that drys hair. We don't want that but sulfates are needed to remove silicones. So we need of shampoos without sulfates and silicones.
